Instructions

Making the Meatballs:

  1. Boil water in pan.
  2. Put ground turkey and half cup of rice in pan.
  3. Put pepper in pan.
  4. Put 4 egg whites in the pan.
  5. Put salt in and mix altogether.
  6. Put water on hands and make turkey balls with mixture.
  7. Put balls in boiling water (donâ€™t make balls too big since rice makes balls bigger).
  8. Put almost high heat.
  9. Put salt in water (balls are ready when rice is popping out and when they float).

Making the Soup:

  1. Rinse serranos/jalapenos.
  2. Get another pan and pour medium amount of oil on pan.
  3. Cut onion to round slices and put these slices in the oil.
  4. Dry serranos and cut the tails off.
  5. Slice the serranos into 4 long pieces.
  6. Cut 2 garlics and put in blender.
  7. Put 1 bay leaf and small thyme branch into blender.
  8. Put 2 full spoons of flour in blender and a bit of a third spoon of flour in blender.
  9. Put 1 tbsp of salt in blender.
  10. Put 1 cilantro and Goya pack seasoning in blender.
  11. Put serranos in with onion in oil and let it cook to release flavor in pan.
  12. Put water in blender and blend in cream (fruit smoothie) setting.
  13. Put soup from blender into pan with onion and serrano (put more water if needed).
  14. Blend soup with spoon.
  15. Peel veggies and cut ends and bad parts from veggies.
  16. Rinse veggies.
  17. Put veggies in soup.
  18. Wait for soup to boil and put meat balls in low heat.
  19. After soup is cooked, put meat balls in soup and serve.
